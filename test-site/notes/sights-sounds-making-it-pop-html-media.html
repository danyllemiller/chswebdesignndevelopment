II. Embedding Video and Audio
The Easy Way (YouTube/Vimeo)
  • Tag Used: An tag creates a "window" to another website inside your own.
  • Benefit: This approach is easier because it offloads the drain of hosting and delivering large video files to the user.
The Hard Way (Native HTML5 Media)

Purpose: Used when you must host the file yourself (e.g., background video loops, proprietary audio).

Media TagRequired AttributeKey Element/Content
<video> (adds the play/pause button, volume, etc.) Uses multiple tags (e.g., .mp4, .webm) to provide browser choices.

1. Source Fallback:

Why do the <video> and <audio> tags use multiple <source> elements (like both .mp4 and .webm or .mp3 and .ogg)?
