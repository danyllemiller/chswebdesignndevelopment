Discipline & Tardies Plan

Classroom Standards & Accountability | Instructor: Ms. Miller

Progressive Discipline

Students must show respect for themselves and others at all times. Any derogatory language or disrespect to peers, instructors, or school staff is unacceptable.

1 Teacher Warning

A brief private meeting outside the classroom to address the issue.

2 Resolution Discussion

Collaborative student/teacher plan of action to resolve the conflict.

3 Parent Contact & Detention

Phone call home and assigned lunchtime detention.

4 Referral Stage

Official referral to Dean or Counselor based on the circumstance.

5 Formal Conference

Required Parent-Teacher-Dean conference to address persistent issues.

*Documentation is maintained for all disciplinary conversations.

Tardies Policy

1st & 2nd: Teacher Warning

3rd & 4th: Call home & Teacher Consequence

5th & 6th: 2 days Lunch Detention (Referral)

7th: 1 day After School Detention

8th+: Parent/Teacher/Dean Conference

Accumulated per class, not per week.

Conduct Logic

Respect is the core of our "Guild." We follow the professional standards found in corporate environments to prepare you for the workplace.
