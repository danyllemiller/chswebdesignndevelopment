The Specificity Score

#id-selector (100 pts)
.class-selector (10 pts)
element-selector (1 pt)

The higher the score, the more "Powerful" the rule. IDs are the heavyweight champs.

Combinator Key

  • 🔹 article p: Descendant (Anywhere inside)
  • 🔹 article > p: Child (Directly inside)
  • 🔹 h1 + p: Next Sibling (Immediately after)
  • 🔹 *: Wildcard (Target Everything)
HEXADECIMAL

#RRGGBB: Base-16 code. Professional standard.

RGB / RGBA

0-255: Intensity of light. Add "A" for Alpha transparency.

HSL / HSLA

Human Centric: Hue (0-360), Saturation (%), Lightness (%).

Pseudo-Classes (States)

  • :hover - Mouse enters element.
  • :focus - Element selected (tabbing).
  • :active - Moment of click.
  • :last-child - Final sibling.

Pseudo-Elements

Inject "Phantom" content without HTML changes.

a::after { content: " 🔗"; }
