About PixelPlay

Who We Are

Welcome to PixelPlay, the premier mobile arcade company bringing the thrill of classic gaming directly to your event! Founded by a team of lifelong gamers, we are passionate about sharing the joy and nostalgia of retro arcade machines with a new generation.

Our fleet of custom-built trucks are equipped with a massive collection of vintage arcade cabinets, from iconic classics like Pac-Man and Space Invaders to competitive fighting games and more. We believe that games are a powerful way to connect people, and we specialize in creating unforgettable experiences for parties, corporate events, and public gatherings.

At PixelPlay, our mission is to make fun accessible. We handle all the logistics, from setup to teardown, so you can focus on what matters most: playing games and making memories. We are dedicated to providing a top-notch, hassle-free experience with a staff of friendly experts ready to help you rediscover your favorite games.
