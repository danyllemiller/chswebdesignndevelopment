Program Description

The Web Design and Development program at CHS provides students with the concepts and technical skills needed to build and maintain professional websites. Our track covers everything from the "Bones" of the web (HTML) and the "Clothes" of visual design (CSS) to the "Brains" of interactivity (JavaScript) and Backend development.

Program Goals

Career Readiness

Gain project-based experience and industry skills needed for entry into tech careers.

Modern Strategies

Master the full design-to-launch cycle using professional tools like VS Code, Figma, and GitHub.

Inclusive Design

Develop websites accessible by everyone, adhering to W3C standards and ADA compliance.

Methods of Instruction

This is a hands-on, computer applications course. Students learn through interactive teacher demonstrations, peer collaboration, and independent quest-based projects. Mastery is demonstrated through the creation of live, functioning web applications.

Program Completer Status

Students who complete the full Web Design CTE pathway unlock significant graduation and college benefits.

Completer Criteria
  • Maintain an average GPA of 3.0 or better in program of study courses.
  • Pass the Workplace Readiness Skills Assessment.
  • Pass the End of Program Assessment.
Completer Rewards

What you get from being a program completer:

  • A red cord to wear at graduation.
  • A College and Career Ready seal on your diploma.
  • An Industry Recognized Credential.
  • You can earn up to nine (9) college credits.
Classroom Tech
  • Design: Adobe Creative Cloud (Photoshop, Illustrator, XD)
  • Development: Visual Studio Code, Git, GitHub
  • Management: Infinite Campus, ParentSquare
  • Environment: Google Workspace
