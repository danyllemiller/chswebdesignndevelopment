PART 1: Welcome to the Manager's Office
  1. What is a CMS?
    • CMS stands for .
    • A true CMS separates the creation from the code structure and design.
    • WordPress is built using the programming language and stores your data in a Database (the "Brain" that remembers everything you type).
  2. The Great Confusion: .com vs .org
    • • WordPress.com (The Rental): A hosting company manages everything for you. It's easy, but you can't install many plugins without paying a high fee. You are living in their house by their rules.
    • • WordPress.org (The Owner): You download the free software and install it on your own server. You have total freedom and own every line of code.
