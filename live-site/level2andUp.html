Web Design II, III & AS

Full-Stack Development | Year 2+

Capstone Description

This course transitions students from front-end designers to full-stack engineers. Students master the "back-end" by connecting applications to live databases and third-party APIs. We emphasize professional version control (Git) and cloud deployment.

Advanced Units

5 Advanced UI/UX & Cloud

Pro Figma prototyping, Git/GitHub collaboration, and Netlify hosting.

6 Persistent Data (SQL)

Relational database architecture and writing SQL queries.

7 CMS Management

WordPress configuration, custom themes, and no-code strategy.

In-Class Internships

Advanced students work with community stakeholders on real projects, such as migrating "Entrepreneurs In Action" to WordPress or building custom apps for the CHS Career Closet.
