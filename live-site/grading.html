Grading Policy

Standards-Based Assessment | Carson High School

Assessment Overview

Grades provide feedback on your progress toward mastering web standards. We use the official Carson High School grading scale across all assessments, including labs, projects, and career readiness tasks.

Web Design I Weights

50% Assignments & Skills: Technical computer-based work and written theory.

20% Projects & Quizzes: Unit exams (written and practical).

20% Semester Exams: Final demonstrations of mastery.

10% Career Readiness: Professionalism, time management, and participation.

Web Design II, III & AS Weights

35% Assignments & Skills: High-level coding and implementation.

35% Projects & Quizzes: Unit projects and research.

20% Semester Exams: Advanced technical applications.

10% Career Readiness: Workplace professionalism and ethics.

Academic Integrity

Plagiarism includes turning in someone else's work as your own or copying exact code without attribution. Students caught plagiarizing (both the giver and the taker) will be given a ZERO.

The Fine Print
  • No Extra Credit: I grade on mastery. Do the assigned work well.
  • Redos: Redo any project until you are happy (within the current or next unit).
  • Life Happens: Request extensions 24 hours in advance via email.
  • Late Penalty: Work is accepted for 60% (D-) after the grace period.
