The Global Infrastructure Roadmap

Physical vs. Logical Layers

THE INTERNET (The Tracks)

Hardware: Fiber Optics, Routers, and Packets. Managed by ISPs and the Backbone.

THE WEB (The Train)

The Service: HTTP, HTML/CSS/JS. Accessed via Browsers. Standardized by the W3C.

An Intranet is a private road; the Internet is a public highway.
