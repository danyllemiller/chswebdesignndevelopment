Forgot your password?

For security compliance, automated password resets are disabled on this network. If you have lost access to your workspace, you must request a Manual Account Reset from the Studio Director (Ms. Miller).

Reset Procedure:
  1. Notify the instructor that you are locked out.
  2. Wait for the instructor to authorize a database wipe for your ID.
  3. Return to the Login page and click Create Account to establish new credentials.
WARNING: A manual account reset will permanently delete all entries in your Digital Notebook. Your grades and HostGator workspace files will remain safe.
