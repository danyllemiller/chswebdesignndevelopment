Carson City School District Policies & Data Compliance

Notice of Non-Discrimination

The Carson City School District does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, or age in its programs and activities. We provide equal access to the Boy Scouts and other designated youth groups per the Equal Access Act of 2002.

Inquiries regarding compliance may be directed to the District Administration office.

Student Data Privacy (SB403 & FERPA)

This portal is an educational tool strictly for the CHS Web Design curriculum. In accordance with the Family Educational Rights and Privacy Act (FERPA) and Nevada SB403:

  • Collection: We collect names, school IDs, and periods solely for roster management.
  • Protection: Student data is never sold or used for commercial profiling/advertising.
  • Access: Information is shared only with authorized school officials for educational purposes.

Cookie Policy

Our site uses necessary session cookies for security and usability:

  • Security: Temporary cookies maintain your login state (expires in 48 hours).
  • UI/UX: Preference cookies remember your screen settings (lasts 1 year).
  • No Tracking: We do not utilize tracking or marketing cookies of any kind.

Your Data Rights

Users may request an exported file of the personal data we hold (grades, notes, files) or request that we erase any personal data upon course completion. This excludes data we are obliged to keep for administrative or legal record-keeping.

Compliance Status

This site is fully compliant with:

  • Nevada SB403 (Student Privacy)
  • NRS 603A (Online Privacy)
  • FERPA (Federal Guidelines)
  • CCSD Technology Policies
